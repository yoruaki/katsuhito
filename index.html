This site serves as a self-introduction, a space for personal experiments, and a portfolio site. The domain "katsuhi.to" cleverly references the webmaster's name, creating a unique "domain hack".

Self-introduction

Hello, my name is Katsuhito Shimaoka, and I am Japanese.

I was born in Yokohama on November 3, 1986, and I have lived here ever since. Currently, I have purchased a second-hand apartment in Yokohama, where I reside with my wife, two sons, and a daughter.

Job and Interests

I am a web developer working at an IT company. The main languages I use are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Recently, I have also become interested in web accessibility.

In addition to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I also actively use modern frameworks and libraries such as Tailwind CSS and Stimulus. These technologies provide a more sophisticated user experience and also contribute to improved maintainability and robustness.

By the way, it is worth noting that we follow the BEM methodology for styling on this site.

Hobbies

Badminton is my favorite hobby. The speed of the game and the thrill of hitting a smash are immensely enjoyable to me. It is my ambition to continue playing badminton throughout my life. As you might already know, I have a deep love for the sport.

Asset management

In addition to working at an IT company, I am also an investor, engaging in stock market investments in both Japan and the United States, real estate investments, and solar power investments. As part of this, I established a private company and became CEO.

My goal is to increase my passive income, aiming for financial independence that is not reliant on manual labor.
